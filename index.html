Why we exist

Powerful tools
belong in the open

Rigorous decision science has long been locked behind enterprise contracts, academic paywalls, and consulting retainers. We are changing that — one carefully crafted open-source package at a time.

Open source as mission

Every Graype package is open because rigorous tools should be freely available to anyone willing to use them well. No paywall. No enterprise tier.

First principles, always

We research, select, and adapt — academic literature before third-party packages, understanding before abstraction. Dependencies are minimised judiciously — not by rule, but because deep understanding rarely needs a borrowed implementation. We own every line we ship.

Freedom with Responsibility

No investor to answer to — but that makes the bar harder, not easier. Every dependency is questioned, every test is earned, every release is a decision — not a deadline. Freedom without that standard is drift. Responsibility without freedom is compliance. Together, they are what makes open-source work worth trusting.

How we sustain the work

Niche consulting.
Serious clients.

Open-source work requires economic oxygen. Ours comes from selective, high-depth consulting engagements with organisations that need genuine quantitative expertise — not headcount.

The arrangement is honest: consulting revenue funds our OSS work. Our OSS work is the proof of capability that earns the consulting engagements. A flywheel we have been spinning for over a decade.

We work with a small number of clients at any time. We say no often. We stay long enough to do the work properly.
