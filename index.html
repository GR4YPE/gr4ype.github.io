Introducing Decisively and Wink

Our open source tool, Decisively, simplifies analytics and decision making. It has gained recognition from the international community: our research papers about Decisively were selected for presentation at the 22nd IEEE Requirements Engineering Conference held at Karlskrona, Sweden in 2014, and the 24th Subjective Probability, Utility, and Decision Making (SPUDM) Conference at IESE Business School, Barcelona in 2013.

Within the Decisively framework, we have designed a family of Machine Learning NPM packages, called Wink. Utilities in Wink can be used independently or combined with others to create powerful solutions for an endless number of problems.
